About Cycle

Cycle founder, Abdalrahman Jarwan

Cycle was an idea that its main point is to help all gamers and PC users to afford PC equipment with high-quality and cheap prices. Abdal and his friends enjoyed video games during their school period. While they were at school, they always would check each other’s equipment’s and sometimes trade to test new products.

Cycle is a project where it’s a safer, easier, and a cheaper way to connect all the users out there to sell, trade, and buy used products. The name Cycle came from the idea where instead of throwing a product you are replacing; others might want to try it. It all goes in a cycle to make sure everyone can use these products. Our mission is to provide affordable products to all users with the satisfaction of the quality needed.
